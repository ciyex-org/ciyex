## 1. Primary Care

**Practice Types**: Family Medicine, Internal Medicine, General Practice

**Popular EHR Systems**: Epic, Cerner (Oracle Health), Athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, NextGen, Practice Fusion

### Patient Chart Tabs

**Dashboard / Summary**
- Patient demographics, photo, preferred name/pronouns
- Active problem list with ICD-10 codes
- Current medications with refill status
- Recent vitals trend graph
- Upcoming appointments
- Outstanding orders/results
- Care gaps / health maintenance alerts
- BMI, smoking status, recent A1C/lipids

**Encounters / Visit Notes**
- SOAP notes (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan)
- Chief complaint
- HPI (History of Present Illness)
- Review of Systems (ROS)
- Physical exam findings
- Assessment with diagnoses
- Plan (orders, prescriptions, referrals, patient education)
- Visit type (office visit, telehealth, phone, annual wellness)
- Time-based or MDM-based E/M coding

**Medical Problems / Conditions**
- Active problem list with ICD-10 codes
- Resolved/inactive problems with dates
- Problem onset date, severity, status
- Associated encounters per problem
- Problem-medication linking

**Medications**
- Active prescriptions with dosage, frequency, route
- Medication history (past medications with stop dates/reasons)
- eRx (electronic prescribing) integration
- Refill requests
- Medication reconciliation
- Prescription drug monitoring program (PDMP) integration
- Drug-drug interaction alerts
- Drug-allergy interaction alerts

**Allergies**
- Drug allergies with reaction type and severity
- Food allergies
- Environmental allergies
- Adverse reactions
- NKDA (No Known Drug Allergies) documentation
- Allergy verification date

**Immunizations**
- Vaccination history with dates, lot numbers, manufacturers
- CDC immunization schedule compliance
- State registry integration (IIS)
- Vaccine administration records
- Due/overdue vaccines
- Contraindications
- VIS (Vaccine Information Statement) documentation

**Lab Results**
- CBC, CMP, lipid panel, A1C, TSH, UA
- Trend graphs for longitudinal values
- Abnormal result flagging
- Reference ranges
- Ordering provider
- External lab integration (Quest, LabCorp)

**Vitals**
- Blood pressure, heart rate, respiratory rate
- Temperature, SpO2
- Height, weight, BMI
- Pain scale
- Head circumference (pediatric)
- Vital trends over time

**Documents / Imaging**
- Scanned documents (consent forms, outside records)
- Imaging results (X-ray, CT, MRI reports)
- DICOM image viewer integration
- Document categorization
- Fax inbox

**Orders**
- Lab orders
- Imaging orders
- Referral orders
- Procedure orders
- DME orders
- Order status tracking

**Referrals**
- Outgoing referrals with reason/urgency
- Referral status tracking
- Specialist reports/feedback
- Prior authorization status

**Billing / Claims**
- CPT codes for visit
- ICD-10 diagnosis codes
- Superbill generation
- Insurance eligibility verification
- Claims submission status
- Co-pay/balance tracking

**Patient Communications**
- Secure messaging (patient portal)
- Phone encounters
- Letters
- Appointment reminders
- Recall notices
- After-visit summaries

**Care Plans / Health Maintenance**
- Preventive care schedule (mammograms, colonoscopies, etc.)
- Chronic disease management protocols
- Annual wellness visit (AWV) checklist
- Screening tools (PHQ-9, GAD-7, AUDIT-C)
- Quality measures tracking (HEDIS, MIPS)

**Family History**
- Family medical history by relationship
- Genetic risk factors
- Family history diagrams

**Social History**
- Smoking/tobacco status (pack-years, quit date)
- Alcohol use (AUDIT-C score)
- Drug use
- Exercise habits
- Diet
- Occupation/employment
- Marital status
- SDOH (Social Determinants of Health): housing, food security, transportation

**Procedures**
- In-office procedures with CPT codes
- Procedure notes
- Consent forms
- Biopsy tracking

## 2. Cardiology

**Popular EHR Systems**: Epic (with Cardiology module), GEMMS One, Lumedx, ScImage PicomEnterprise, Merge Cardio

### Patient Chart Tabs

**Dashboard / Summary**
- Cardiac risk scores (Framingham, ASCVD)
- EF (Ejection Fraction) trend
- Active cardiac diagnoses
- Device summary (pacemaker/ICD)
- Anticoagulation status
- Recent cardiac imaging summary

**Encounters / Visit Notes**
- Cardiology-specific templates
- Chest pain evaluation
- Heart failure management
- Arrhythmia follow-up
- Post-procedure follow-up

**Cardiac Diagnostics**
- ECG/EKG with interpretation
- Echocardiogram reports (TTE, TEE)
- Stress testing (treadmill, nuclear, stress echo, pharmacologic)
- Cardiac catheterization reports
- Electrophysiology studies
- Cardiac MRI / CT angiography
- Holter monitor / event monitor results
- Ambulatory blood pressure monitoring

**Hemodynamic Data**
- Cardiac output / cardiac index
- Pulmonary artery pressures
- Wedge pressure
- Systemic vascular resistance
- Mixed venous oxygen saturation

**Device Management**
- Pacemaker/ICD interrogation reports
- Device type, manufacturer, model, serial number
- Lead placement and thresholds
- Battery status
- Programming history
- Remote monitoring data
- Device alerts

**Anticoagulation Management**
- INR tracking with dosing history
- Target INR range
- Anticoagulant type (warfarin, DOACs)
- Bleeding risk scores (HAS-BLED)
- CHA2DS2-VASc score
- Bridging protocols

**Cardiac Rehabilitation**
- Exercise prescription
- Metabolic equivalent (MET) levels
- Session logs
- Progress tracking
- Risk stratification

**Heart Failure Management**
- NYHA classification
- Daily weight tracking
- Fluid balance
- BNP/NT-proBNP trends
- Diuretic titration
- Cardiac resynchronization therapy

**Vascular Studies**
- Carotid duplex
- Peripheral arterial studies (ABI)
- Venous duplex
- Aortic imaging

- Gait analysis
- Romberg test
- Plantar responses

**Seizure / Epilepsy**
- Seizure diary (type, frequency, duration, triggers)
- Antiepileptic drug levels
- EEG reports
- Video-EEG monitoring summaries
- Seizure classification (ILAE)

- VNS (vagus nerve stimulator) programming

**Headache / Migraine**
- Headache diary (frequency, severity, triggers, aura)
- MIDAS disability score
- HIT-6 score
- Preventive medication tracking
- Acute treatment log
- Botox injection sites for chronic migraine

**Stroke**
- NIHSS (National Institutes of Health Stroke Scale) scoring
- Modified Rankin Scale
- Neurovascular imaging (CT angiography, MRA)
- tPA administration records
- Thrombectomy records
- Secondary prevention measures

**Movement Disorders**
- UPDRS (Unified Parkinson's Disease Rating Scale)
- Hoehn and Yahr staging
- DBS (Deep Brain Stimulator) programming
- Medication on/off timing
- Tremor rating scales
- Video documentation of movements

**Multiple Sclerosis**
- EDSS (Expanded Disability Status Scale)
- MRI lesion tracking (new/enhancing lesions)
- Disease-modifying therapy (DMT) history
- Relapse documentation
- JCV antibody status
- OCT (optical coherence tomography) results

**Cognitive / Dementia**
- MMSE, MoCA scores
- Neuropsychological testing results
- Functional assessment
- Behavioral symptoms (NPI)
- Caregiver assessment
- Advance directive status

**EMG/NCS**
- Nerve conduction study results
- Electromyography findings
- Neuromuscular junction testing
